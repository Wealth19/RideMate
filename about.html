About RideMate

Who We Are

RideMate is a campus transportation booking system designed to help students get safe and affordable rides. It reduces the stress of transportation by connecting students with verified drivers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve student mobility by delivering a secure and reliable ride booking solution that ensures comfort, safety, and affordability.

Our Services

  • Online ride booking for students and staff
  • Verified driver registration and monitoring
  • Ride history and tracking support
  • Affordable and fixed pricing system
  • Customer support and complaint handling

How RideMate Works

Students register, request a ride, enter pickup and destination details, and a verified driver is assigned to them. Payments can be done through card or transfer.
